Welcome to Oookbook

What is Oookbook?

A website, where you can post a library catalogue of your paper books, browse through libraries of others and borrow their books. The system tries to be as simple as possible with one-click solution wherever possible.

Sign In Sign Up

What is Oookbook not?

  • An eBook sharing site.
  • A book selling/buying site.
  • A book database.
  • A book review site.
  • A social network.
